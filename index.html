Who We Are

Pioneering Next-Gen Diagnostics & Supply

10+ Years of Clinical Excellence

Blessed Laboratory Services & Supply is a premier healthcare institution and direct laboratory equipment supplier headquartered in Siaya, Kenya. Led by experienced Laboratory Technologist & Director Wanga Caroline, we bridge the gap between complex diagnostic technology and accessible patient care.
